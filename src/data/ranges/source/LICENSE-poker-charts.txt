This directory contains preflop range chart data vendored from the open-source
project "poker-charts" by Anton Safonov:

    https://github.com/AHTOOOXA/poker-charts

Used under the MIT License (reproduced below). The data in pekarstas.ts is the
"pekarstas" (GGPoker) chart pack from that project, re-encoded into this app's
RangeNode schema by ../convert.ts. No modifications were made to the strategy
itself.
